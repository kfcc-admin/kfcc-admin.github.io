Heritage

The Korean Family Counseling & Research Center (KFCC) was founded with a deep commitment to serving Korean families and communities. Our organization has been built on a foundation of understanding, respect, and dedication to the unique needs of Korean families navigating life in a new cultural context.

Since our establishment, KFCC has been at the forefront of providing comprehensive support services that address the multifaceted challenges faced by Korean families. Our heritage is rooted in the values of family unity, education, and community empowerment. We believe that by strengthening families, we strengthen entire communities, creating a ripple effect of positive change that extends far beyond our immediate reach.

Over the years, KFCC has evolved to meet the changing needs of our community while staying true to our core mission of advancing social mobility and providing essential services that enable families to thrive.

Mission Statement

The Korean Family Counseling & Research Center (KFCC) is dedicated to advancing social mobility and empowering Korean families through comprehensive support services, education, and community development initiatives.

Our mission is to provide accessible legal support, deliver high-quality education and training programs, and establish community centers that serve as hubs for growth, connection, and advancement. We are committed to breaking down barriers and creating pathways to success for individuals and families in our community.

Legal Support

We provide essential legal assistance to help families navigate complex legal systems, understand their rights, and access justice. Our legal support services ensure that community members have the resources they need to protect themselves and their families.

Education & Training Programs

Our comprehensive education and training programs equip community members with the skills, knowledge, and confidence needed to succeed. From language classes to professional development workshops, we offer pathways to personal and professional growth.

Community Center Establishment

We establish and maintain community centers that serve as gathering places for connection, learning, and support. These centers provide a physical space where families can access resources, participate in programs, and build lasting relationships within the community.

Through these core services, KFCC works tirelessly to create opportunities for social mobility and ensure that every family in our community has the tools and support they need to achieve their goals and build a brighter future.
